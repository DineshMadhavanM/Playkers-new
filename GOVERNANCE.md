# Governance

## Roles
- Maintainers: Review PRs, triage issues, manage releases.
- Contributors: Open issues/PRs following CONTRIBUTING.md.

## Decision making
- Minor changes: Maintainers merge after one approval.
- Major changes: Open an RFC issue for discussion and approval.

## Becoming a maintainer
- Demonstrate sustained, high-quality contributions.
- Be nominated by an existing maintainer.

## Code of Conduct
- See CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md. Violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions.
